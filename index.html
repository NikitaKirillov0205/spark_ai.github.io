/ ABOUT

Our mission is to make people's life better, no matter their health condition. This application is created for people with Parkinson's disease. Our goal was to make the lifestyle of sick people more comfortable, despite their illness. We will mention that this website is not for people with the disease, but for their close people who can help them. Here we will tell you about our progress, ideas and obviously how to use our product.
